# MTN Cloud Streaming Platform

MCSP is a large-scale, multi-tenant SaaS streaming service built exclusively on MTN cloud infrastructure. It delivers video and audio content to Nigerian domestic audiences and the international diaspora under a dual-tier monetisation model: ad-supported free viewing and an ad-free paid subscription.
